# Refund Policy
Last updated: [30/04/2024]

Because BuildIT handles digital goods and escrow, refunds follow a strict process.

# 1. When Will You Get a Refund?
Seller fails to deliver any code after 48 hours of payment confirmation.

Delivered content is not as described (e.g., different product, empty file).

Order is marked disputed and admin decides in buyer’s favour.

# 2. When Will You NOT Get a Refund?
You changed your mind after the seller has already delivered working code.

You failed to open a dispute within 7 days after payment.

The order was auto‑released because you did not respond for 48 hours (buyer’s responsibility).

The transaction succeeded on the blockchain but you sent the wrong amount (random suffix mismatch).

You attempted to charge back or reverse a confirmed blockchain transaction.

# 3. Refund Amount
Only the base price (without the random suffix) is refunded to the buyer.

The platform retains the overpaid suffix as a processing fee.

Refunds are sent manually by the admin, usually within 7 days after the dispute is resolved.

# 4. How to Request a Refund
Use the Report Problem button inside the order.

Provide clear evidence (screenshots, files, chat logs).

Wait for admin review. Do not try to charge back via your wallet – that is impossible once confirmed.

Important: These policies apply only to transactions made through BuildIT. The platform owner reserves the right to update them with reasonable notice
