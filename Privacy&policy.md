#  Privacy Policy
Last updated: [30/04/2026]

This Privacy Policy explains what data BuildIT collects and how it is used.

# 1. Data We Collect
Discord User ID – to identify you in the database.

Transaction History – orders, payments, deliveries, withdrawals.

Listings – titles, descriptions, prices you post.

IP address – stored in server logs (not used by the bot directly).

We do not collect email addresses, real names, or private keys.

# 2. How We Use Your Data
To process orders, calculate commissions, and manage escrow.

To provide support tickets and dispute resolution.

To display your balance and order history.

# 3. Data Sharing
We do not sell or rent your data.

We may share limited data (Discord ID, order details) with the counterparty of a transaction (buyer/seller).

We may disclose data if required by law.

# 4. Data Retention
Your data is kept as long as your account is active.

When you delete your Discord account or request removal, we will anonymise your data within 30 days, except for transaction logs required for financial records (kept for 1 year).

# 5. Security
We use PDO prepared statements and hashed API keys.

However, no system is 100% secure. You accept the inherent risks of using any online service.

# 6. Your Rights
You can request a copy of your data by contacting the bot owner.

You can delete your data by asking an admin to remove your user record.

Important: These policies apply only to transactions made through BuildIT. The platform owner reserves the right to update them with reasonable notice.
